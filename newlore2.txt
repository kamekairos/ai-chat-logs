The Realm of Terra: A Literary Overview

Here's a breakdown of your world, organized for clarity:
I. Cosmology & Realms

    The Three Worlds: The universe is structured into three primary interconnected realms:
        Terra (The Mortal Realm/Land of the Living): This is the central plane, analogous to Earth. It's where humans and many other races like the Papillion and Beastfolk reside. Kingdoms like Freyhaven are located here.
        The Heavens: The domain of the Goddess and angels like Amaryllis and (formerly) Inari. Light arcane magic is directly tied to the Goddess's power.
        The Underworld (Niflheim - Capital City): Ruled by Hades and Persephone. It's the realm of the dead, dark angels (like Inari after her fall), vampires (like Viktoryia), and beings like the Draugr and Reapers (like Nyx).
    Yggdrasil (The Tree of Wisdom):
        Source of All Magic & Power: All magical energy in the three worlds stems from Yggdrasil.
        Binder of Worlds: This colossal tree connects and binds Terra, the Heavens, and the Underworld.
        Kept Alive by the Goddess: The Goddess dedicates her own energy to sustain Yggdrasil.
        Guardians: The High Elves of the northern forests (e.g., Kai) are the guardians of Yggdrasil.
        Health as a Metric: The health and state of Yggdrasil directly reflect the balance of energy and power across the realms. Mana molecules from Yggdrasil are consumed/stored by beings like Dryads.

II. Key Powers & Factions

    The Heavens:
        Ruler: The Goddess.
        Inhabitants/Forces: Angels (High Seraphim like Amaryllis, lower-level angels like Niomae).
    The Underworld:
        Rulers: Hades (King) and Persephone (Queen).
        Inhabitants/Forces: Dark Angels (like Inari), Vampires (like Viktoryia), Reapers (like Nyx), Draugr (powerful ghoul military force maintaining order, controlled by Nyx under Hades & Persephone's discretion).
    Terra:
        Current Overarching Ruler: The Mad Monarch and his oligarchy (an allegory for Donald Trump and wealthy elites). This regime is cruel and oppressive.
        Freyhaven: A diverse and prosperous kingdom known as a stronghold of freedom (allegory for the United States). It houses the Academy of Arcane Arts.
        Papiliona Silvana (Papiliona): A forest kingdom of the Papillion race, ruled by their royal court (Princess Luminosa is heir).
    Dragonkin Aristocracy: An ancient, powerful, and rare race with deep ties to Yggdrasil, living secluded in mountains.

III. Races & Peoples

    Humans: Became more plentiful after the Thousand Year War. Reside in Terra (e.g., Momohana, Aodhagán, Vivienne).
    Angels:
        High Seraphim: (e.g., Amaryllis) Powerful angels from the Heavens.
        Dark Angels: (e.g., Inari) Angels aligned with or residing in the Underworld, often with a fallen status.
        Low-Level Angels: (e.g., Niomae) Angels with developing powers.
    Gods/Goddesses: Powerful entities ruling their respective realms (The Goddess, Hades, Persephone).
    Mages: Practitioners of magic, often students of arcane arts (e.g., Aodhagán, Vivienne).
    Beastfolk: Humanoid creatures often shunned and persecuted, especially under the Mad Monarch. Non-registered beastfolk are exiled to the Wastelands. (e.g., Anastasia, Cecelia the squirrel girl).
    Papillion Race: Humanoids residing in the forest of Papiliona Silvana, known for healing powers. Women of the royal court undergo intensive academy training. (e.g., Princess Luminosa/Lulu).
    Orcs: Increasing in number and aggression, patrolling near Papiliona Silvana since the Mad Monarch took power.
    Elves: One of the races that hunted dragons.
        High Elves: Guardians of Yggdrasil (e.g., Kai).
    Dwarves: One of the races that hunted dragons.
    Gnomes: One of the races that hunted dragons.
    Dragonkin: Ancient, powerful humanoid race with dragon features (horns, wings, tail) and the ability to transform into dragons. Very few remain, living secluded. Deep connection to Yggdrasil. (e.g., Zephyra).
    Dragons: Extremely rare, majestic creatures, hunted almost to extinction. Sightings are omens of great change. Can sense pure intentions. (e.g., Zephyra in her dragon form).
    Vampires: Ancient, immortal beings. (e.g., Viktoryia).
    Draugr: Powerful ghouls forming the military/law enforcement of the Underworld. Controlled by Nyx.
    Reapers: Beings associated with death. (e.g., Nyx, the "dreaded reaper").
    Dryads: Tree nymphs from Greek mythology, born from trees and tied to their life. They store mana from Yggdrasil in their chests.
